Assignment 4 | CSS Grid, Flexbox, Column-cunt, and Column-rule

Homework 4 requires the use of work files hw4part1.html, hw4part2.html, and fairytale.html. Hw4part1.html has you rely on grid to modify the page. Hw4part2.html has you rely on flexbox to modify the page layout. Finally, fairytale.html has you work with column-count and column-rule. All three are methods for outlining a webpage. View Assignment 7 - CCS3 Animation

For Assignment 7, Part 1, you are tasked with creating a CSS3 animation of a flying airplane using a .png of a plane. The page must include a centered and static heading for the company name and an animated banner reading “FLY WITH US, WE ARE SAFE” that enters the left and exits to the right and finishes in the center of the screen. The banner’s text is also animated. In Part 2, you are tasked with designing another webpage that includes a main heading, a subheading, and at least two paragraphs that wrap around a .png of a house. You need to rely on the shape-outside property to accomplish this assignment. You also need to use a container query to reduce the h2 size when its container is under 600px wide.

The second page is for your Google Analytics Report analysis. You’ll download a meaningful Analytics report in PDF format (such as user acquisition or traffic behavior), upload it to your site, and write a professional analysis of at least 150 words. This analysis should explain what the report shows and suggest ways to improve your website based on the data. The page must also include a clearly worded hyperlink to your PDF report and follow good SEO practices introduced in Part 1.

  • Homework 7 Part Two - CSS shape-outside property
